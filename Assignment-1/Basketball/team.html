About the Team

The India men's national basketball team represents India in international men's basketball. It is controlled by Basketball Federation of India.[3] A 1936 founding member of FIBA Asia,[4] India has one of Asia's longest basketball traditions. Throughout its history, Team India qualified for the FIBA Asia Championship 24 times and is placed in the top-5 in appearances in this tournament.
